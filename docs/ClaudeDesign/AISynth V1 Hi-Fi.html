01 · What changed

OSC 1 and OSC 2 now stack vertically in a narrower left column. Each row shows the wave scope next to a 4-knob row — tight, readable, no dead space.

The right column gets the breathing room: Filter with a live response curve, then the Amp and Filter envelopes as big, directly-draggable visualizers.

02 · AI flow

Click prompt top-right. A pull-down opens with a text field, generates 4 sound variations, and you pick one. The selected variation becomes the live patch and auto-saves into History.

03 · History

Every prompt is a preset. Click History in the tab bar to see the full log — prompt text, timestamp, tags, and one-click recall. Prev/Next arrows at the top cycle through it without leaving the editor.
